How To Join

Becoming a member of the IEEE is easy! For our organization, Fresno State students just need to join both IEEE’s national organization and our local chapter.

Joining Nationally

For student members of IEEE, the member dues required are substantially less than those of professionals. As of the 2019 membership dues cycle, student full-year dues are a meager $32.00! To take advantage of this discount, first visit this landing page on the IEEE.org website. The website should prepare a webpage to where you must create your user account.

Once you have created your account and your member dues on the national level have been accounted for, you will be given an IEEE Number. This is your unique ID as an IEEE member and is a requirement for all members to have within our local chapter. To locate your IEEE ID, visit your personal profile page located here. In the Member/Customer Number field, you will locate your ID number.

Joining Locally

The local Fresno State chapter offers many services to its members, including free printing, complimentary coffee, snack bar credits during finals, and opportunities to attend extremely valuable conferences, trips, and events. Joining the local chapter requires your IEEE National ID and a members due fee of $10 as of Fall 2019. This fee is to help accommodate costs for printing, maintenance of the room, and improving the well-being of the club. Visit the IEEE room located at EE226 and request for a club officer to help vouch and verify your payment. Upon payment, you will sign your name on our IEEE Membership list, and you will be good to go!
